Drawy Finger

Drawy Finger is an app that lets you draw things with your finger. It was created because the client recognized the difficulty and inconvenience of drawing with one's toes. Using Drawy Finger allows the artist the great time-saver of not needing to remove the shoes and socks for each drawing session.
